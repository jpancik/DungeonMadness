What's the game about?

Dungeon Madness is an “almost oldschool” dungeon crawler in which you gain control of a young hero Hero (he actually doesn’t have a name, but I felt sorry for him so I gave him this one right now). Instead of reading about it, why don’t you try it for yourself? You can download it here. If you don’t feel like playing take a short look at our gallery instead.
