What's the game about?

It's an "almost oldschool" dungeon crawler in which you gain control of a young hero. Purpose of his life is to get to the lowest floor of the dungeon. He just has to go deeper.
Works on Linux, Mac and Windows. Requires Opengl 2.0 and Java.
